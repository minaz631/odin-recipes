Meatballs

This Classic Meatballs recipe is an absolute favorite, and for great reason! All it takes to make tender, perfectly browned meatballs is 15 minutes of prep, 8 basic ingredients and a foil-lined pan. !

Bake to perfection, and these classic Italian meatballs could be the answer to tonight's dinner jam! Serve them with spaghetti or other noodles, rice, veggies, salad or any of your favorite dishes. This recipe truly goes with anything

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Bread crumps
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Milk
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Egg

Steps
